Why?

While a short term crisis relief is needed today, we want to look ahead and prepare everything, so it can start rebuilding as soon as the war is won. For this, we need a clear roadmap and fast funding for the most efficient initiatives. With the Upgrade Ukraine summits, we will provide both:

Upgrade Ukraine Summit is structured in 2 phases

Upgrade Ukraine Summit 1

for bringing civil society actors together to develop a midterm plan for different action areas

September 2022

near Berlin

Upgrade Ukraine Summit 2

for direct investment of foundations, investorts, family offices, public funds to realize the most important and needed Ukrainian rebuilding projects

November 2022

Berlin

We create a vision of Ukraine even during the war because we beleive in victory. Our Purpose is to create a better Ukraine for future generations.

On the first Summit in September 2022, we will gather a Committee of 100 Ukrainian and non-Ukrainian Experts to develop the roadmap (for rebuilding Ukraine in each of 9 focus areas).

This roadmap will be the basis to map and select promising projects in each area and connect them with investors at the second summit in November 2022.
