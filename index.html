Upgrade Ukraine is an initiative aiming to ensure that promised funds and support are used for a value-driven recovery linking Ukrainian potential with international resources.

Engage

We wish to create a better Ukraine

For future generations by connecting rapid crisis response with the demonstration of systemic change and long-term impact. Everyone is invited to dream beyond the pre-war status quo.

Upgrade Ukraine Summit is structured in 4 phases

On June 23rd 2022, Ukraine was granted European Union Candidate Status. During the next 500 days, we will organize Upgrade Ukraine as a process that enables the development of tactical alliances, shared taxonomies, and collaborative interventions through a series of events:

Summit “Directing”

Compass & Upgrade principles

10/2022

Summit “Ideating”

Missions & ideas for systemic demonstrations

01/2023

Summit “Matching”

04/2023

Festival in Ukraine “Scaling”

07/2023
